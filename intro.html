Objectives:

Key questions related to the average screen time of high school students include: How much time do high school students typically spend on screens, including smartphones, computers, and other digital devices, on a daily and weekly basis? What are the driving factors behind variations in screen time? By addressing these questions, the research aims to provide insights into the average screen time of high school students and its implications for their education and well-being.
